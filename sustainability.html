Sustainability

Coca-Cola is committed to building a sustainable future, from reducing plastic waste to investing in renewable energy.

Our Sustainability Goals

We strive to collect and recycle the equivalent of every bottle or can we sell globally by 2030. Our goal is to create a world without waste.

Water Stewardship

Water is essential for our business and our communities. We are dedicated to replenishing the water we use in our beverages. Through community projects, we aim to return to nature and communities an amount of water equivalent to what we use in our beverages.

Climate Action

We are committed to reducing our carbon footprint across our entire supply chain. By improving energy efficiency and investing in renewable energy sources, we aim to significantly lower our greenhouse gas emissions.

Waste Management

Our goal is to ensure that all packaging is recyclable by 2025. We are also focused on promoting recycling in communities and working to reduce the amount of plastic waste.

Community Engagement

We invest in the communities we serve through various initiatives aimed at improving health, education, and economic empowerment. Together, we can create a sustainable future.

Sustainable Agriculture

Our agricultural supply chain is essential to producing many of our beverages. We work with farmers to promote sustainable farming practices, ensuring responsible sourcing of sugar, coffee, tea, and other ingredients.

Renewable Energy Investments

In addition to energy efficiency, we are investing in renewable energy projects such as wind and solar power to reduce our dependency on fossil fuels. This is a vital part of reducing our environmental impact globally.

Packaging Innovation

We are continuously innovating to create more sustainable packaging. By introducing plant-based bottles and reducing the plastic in our bottles, we are driving packaging solutions that are better for the environment.

Global Partnerships for a Better Planet

We collaborate with global organizations and governments to promote sustainability. These partnerships focus on projects that address key environmental challenges such as ocean pollution, deforestation, and water conservation.
